Rules

  • No limitations on team size
  • NO solution sharing is allowed
  • Attacking our infrastructure is prohibited (only attack designated services)
  • Attacking other teams is prohibited
  • Do not DOS, it will result in an immediate ban.
  • Should you find any vulnerability/bug in our scoreboard or infrastructure please report it to us, bonus points will be awarded
  • If yout think you've got a valid flag but the scoreboard is not accepting it please contact us.
  • The first 3 solvers for challenges get bonus points. 6%, 4% and 2% of the challenge value.
  • TowerOfHanoi affiliated individuals or groups cannot claim prizes
  • to claim prizes you will need to have a valid point-of-contact
