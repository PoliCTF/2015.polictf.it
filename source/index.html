PoliCTF 2015 is a jeopardy style capture-the-flag competition that will be held both online and open to all competitors and live for a restricted number of participants. The competition will start during the second day of the DIMVA 2015 conference that will be held in Milan (Italy) at 10/07/2015 09:00 UTC and will last for 48 hours. Our official communication channel is #polictf on hackint irc network.
